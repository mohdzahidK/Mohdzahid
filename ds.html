NFL Football Stats (defense) Explorer



This app performs simple webscraping of NFL Football player stats data (focusing on defenseS)! Python libraries: base64, pandas, streamlit, numpy, matplotlib, seaborn Data source: [pro-football-reference.com](https://www.pro-football-reference.com/)
